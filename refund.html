Refund Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

At SWAZ, we are committed to your satisfaction. This Refund Policy explains when and how you may be eligible for a refund on your subscription.

💡 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee — If you are not satisfied with SWAZ for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your first payment and we will issue a full refund, no questions asked.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

You are eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your initial subscription payment
  • You have not previously received a refund for a SWAZ subscription
  • Your account has not been suspended or terminated for violations of our Terms of Service

2. Renewals and Subsequent Billing Cycles

Refunds are generally not provided for renewal charges after the initial 30-day period. If you wish to cancel, please do so before your next renewal date to avoid being charged. You can cancel at any time from your account settings.

Exceptions may be made at our discretion in cases of accidental renewal (within 48 hours of the charge) or documented technical issues preventing access to the platform.

3. Annual Plans

If you purchased an annual plan, you are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, we may offer a pro-rated refund for the remaining unused months on a case-by-case basis.

4. Free Plan Users

Our free tier is provided at no cost. There are no charges associated with the free plan, so no refunds apply.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@wswaz.io with your registered email address and the date of your purchase. We aim to process all refund requests within 5–7 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

6. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks can result in account suspension. We are always happy to resolve billing issues directly and quickly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or want to request a refund, please reach out at support@wswaz.io.
