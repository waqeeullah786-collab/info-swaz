Common questions about pricing
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account dashboard at any time. You won't be charged again and keep access until your billing period ends.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes — both Pro and Business plans include a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal via our secure Stripe-powered checkout.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Absolutely. You can switch plans anytime. Upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.
