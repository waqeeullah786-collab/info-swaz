Our Story

From a single workflow
to a full SaaS platform.

SWAZ started with a simple frustration: why do sellers still spend hours doing research that software could do in seconds? We set out to fix that.

We're a product of Waqee Global Tech — a software and AI automation company based in Islamabad, Pakistan. Our platform is powered by real-time data pipelines, AI scoring engines, and deep marketplace intelligence.

Today, SWAZ helps 20,000+ sellers on Amazon and Walmart discover profitable niches faster, reduce research errors, and make smarter product decisions every day.

We're not just engineers — we're sellers and product people who obsess over every data point and user workflow that SWAZ delivers.
